Who We Are

We Inject Liquidity into Financial Markets

As a leading financial institution, we are here to inject liquidity into financial markets, enabling all participants. We buy and sell securities from emerging markets as well as established economies. We trade cryptocurrencies, fixed income, FX, equities and ETFs.

We have started with FX in our early years and after generating a lot of alpha we grew exponentially, leading us to diversify into other asset classes such as equities and commodities. We are now more and more interested in digital assets.

Market Dominance with Cutting Edge Technology

We are technical to the core and have a strong relationship with leading research institutions within top universities. We utilise a variety of sophisticated machine learning algorithms to analyse large volumes of different types of data in order to capture relationships between exogenous and endogenous variables. This will help us make better trading decisions in a bid to generate alpha for our stakeholders.

Our core differentiating factor is combining sophisticated mathematical models with acumen and savvy of seasoned trading professionals to bring us closer to what we call a human machine design pattern that help us maximise our core capabilities.

Approach

Our Approach to Tackling Challenges

As a technology first quantitative trading firm, tech is ingrained in our every fabric. We process different types of data to generate meaningful signals and remove noise.

Our team of quantitative researchers, quantitative developers and software engineers work hand in hand with our seasoned traders to turn the signals, results of our dogged research, into trading opportunities.

Such innovative multi-dimensional approach to solving complex problems helps us stay at the forefront of innovation.

Culture

Our Culture Keeps Us Ahead of the Competition

Our office environment feels like home. We have managed to combine the rigour of academic settings with the structure of established financial institutions as well as the laid-back attitude of tech firms.

Our company is a beautiful synergy of all. We are a people first flat organisation with plenty of room to grow intellectually.
