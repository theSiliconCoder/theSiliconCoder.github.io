Internships

Intern With Us

We have different on-cycle and off-cycle internship opportunities within QCL. These are available to university students who are doing their 2nd year of studies in a STEM discipline such as computer science, mathematics, physics and engineering at a leading institution.

You should have a knack for problem solving and enjoy working in a fast paced environment.

Jobs

Work With Us

If you are a recent university graduate from a STEM discipline and at a leading establishment, feel free to apply to one of our roles. We periodically advertise roles within our quant research, quant development and quant trading divisions.

As a quant trader, a STEM degree from a top university is required, and so is programming skills in at least one programming language, preferably Python or C++. Basic understanding of finance and trading can also help.

What We Expect From You

As a quant dev and a quant researcher, you are expected to have a solid understanding of mathematical modelling, computer programming, especially Python, C++, SQL and ideally a functionally programming language such as Haskell. Advanced understanding of machine learning techniques is required for quant research roles.
